About Joe Hood

I’m a Cambridge local with a lifelong passion for property and a career that spans every corner of the sector. From managing branches to overseeing refurbishments, lettings, and sales, I’ve built a strong foundation of experience and a reputation for delivering results. Before property, I played professional football, an experience that shaped my work ethic, drive, and commitment to high standards. I bring that same focus and discipline into every deal, ensuring clients receive exceptional service from start to finish.

The property market is changing fast. I believe success today requires a fresh, forward-thinking approach, and I pride myself on being adaptable, efficient, and always one step ahead. I take the time to understand each client’s individual goals and tailor my approach to meet them, making sure every decision is strategic and well-informed. By combining hands-on experience with modern methods and market insight, I aim to maximise value and minimise risk at every stage of the process. Whether you’re investing, selling, or letting, my goal is simple: to make the process seamless and deliver outstanding outcomes every time.
